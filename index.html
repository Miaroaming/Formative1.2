ABOUT US

Welcome to Loosies Superette, where there's always something to tickle your taste buds and your wallet.

Our venue is fully accessible to persons with disabilities, but our downstairs speakeasy, with a second bar and pool tables, is inaccessible to wheelchair users due to the building's structure.

So whether you're stopping by for a quick bite, a game of pool, we've got you covered. Grab a seat, grab a burger, and let the good times roll!
